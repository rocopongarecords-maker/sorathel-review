# VAELEN — Senior Practitioner, Pattern Reader

## Physical
- Tall — noticeably, has to angle shoulders through standard doorways
- Long face, dark-eyed, handsome in a landscape way (not pretty, not symmetrical, but arranged so you keep looking)
- Faint lines at corners of mouth
- Shadows under eyes (fatigue or permanent)
- Past thirty; energy reads younger — quick, magnetic, slightly restless

## Behavior on Page
- Eats Ashara smokebread one-handed through chaos
- Arm slung over carriage side with tavern-chair looseness
- Grin that transforms his face — softens angles, warms eyes, makes people feel like the only person in the room
- When reading the network: face empties, head tilts, mechanical precision — then snaps back seamlessly
- Left hand closes into a fist when worried — the only part of him that doesn't perform ease

## Gift / Competence
- Extracted 18 months of node data from a 3-second unfocused stare at 200 meters
- Identified soil depletion pattern, Mirathyn overconsumption, downstream compensation
- Read Drenna's frontier origin from her boots/posture/walk pattern
- [Ch 2] Identified exact spot and timing of field failure by reading "scars" in the network
- [Ch 3] Discovered the downstream pull (40+ miles south-southwest) and the anomaly in the silence between pulses
- Can redirect Amarvel flow through branch work — raised ground temperature across settlement from a trunk junction

## Deep Reading Cost
- Headache behind left eye, metallic taste, hand tremors
- Getting worse with repeated use — "It took longer than it used to"
- Surface reads easy (walking, talking); deep reads replace his senses entirely

## The Warmth
- The warmth IS genuine. He discovered at sixteen that his natural warmth could be deployed deliberately
- He's been doing both — feeling and deploying — ever since
- He can't always tell which is which
- The tragedy isn't a broken performer — it's an extraordinary person who can't see that the extraordinary thing isn't the performance

## The Unguarded Space
- His love for the Amarvel — raw, helpless, the one thing about him that isn't managed
- Slipped out once during branch work with Caeleth: "It is." Then the door closed again

## Key Relationships
- **Sorvyn:** Complete trust that feels like gravity. Vaelen thinks: "he trusts me completely and I am not at all certain I deserve it"
- **Drenna:** She looks past the warmth. She told him something about his own gift nobody ever had. He admitted "I don't know how to do that" — the most honest thing he's said in months
- **Caeleth:** Sees his younger self. "I was you. I remember being you. I remember the part that came after."
- **Jorath:** Warm teasing; assigns him work that plays to his strengths

## What the Reader Doesn't Know Yet
- The full wound, the serial chase pattern, what broke him, his relationship history
- The ceiling — too good for deployments, not brave enough to reach for more
- The creature rapport (Season 3 warging/bond seed)

## Voice (for POV chapters)
Fast, restless, always processing. Notices everything automatically. The control visible from inside — choosing warmth, producing the right response. But underneath: raw sensory overwhelm with the Amarvel. His one unguarded space.
