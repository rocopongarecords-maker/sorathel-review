## Resourcefulness
- Survived a 40-foot crevasse fall (Veldryn deployment) — first words were about hazard pay
- Instant shift from accounting mode to professional when Vaelen reports node stress
- **The land-skiff project:** Building a mechanical land-skiff in a rented workshop on Cartway Street (lower city). Ironwood frame with forged metal brackets, pedal-driven chain-and-gear mechanism for low speed, collapsible sail mast for open road. Rear wheels salvaged from decommissioned hauler; front wheels built from scratch for tiller steering. Suspension springs sourced from Orvel (8 months of searching). In a creature-bonded world, this is eccentric bordering on insane. People think he's lost his mind. He doesn't care. The project is PURE JOY — he'd never call it that, he'd call it "not being bored." Economical with mundane purchases; spends freely on the skiff (rejected cheaper springs because the temper was wrong). Quality non-negotiable here. Seeds Zethael's independent-of-Amarvel tech.

## Voice (for POV chapters)
Precise. Transactional. Finds comfort in numbers and measurements. Humor through complaint and dry observation. He notices what things cost, what they're made of, how they fit together.
